Economic Communities in Africa

AU:
African Union - A continental organization made up of African countries. Founded in July 9,2002 replacing OAU
EAC:
East African Community- Regional organization made up of East African countries that work together to promote free trade, common policies and reqional development
SADC:
Southern African Development Community - Regional organization of Southern African Countries that work together for regional development, peace and security
ECOWAS:
Economic Community for West African States - Group for west African countires for purposes of promoting economic integration, peace and development
